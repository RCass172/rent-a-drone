Hi my name is Robert Shaw and I have been a professional photographer for the last 10 years. I have done many professional shoots over years and loved every minute of it, but I wanted to bring it to the next level. That's when I discovered the drone. For the last 3 years I have developed a love for my drone photography and wanted to share my new-found skills with others.
To anyone wanting to capture a special moment in their lives, I can rent my drone and piloting skills along with my photography skills for a day, or if you feel comfortable flying the drone yourself I can also rent the drone out for the day
I've captured hikes, weddings, baby showers, surprise engagements, just to name a few. Contact me if you have any questions or ideas you'd like to talk through and i would be happy to help. Also check out my Instagram page to see more of what I've captured throughout the years.
